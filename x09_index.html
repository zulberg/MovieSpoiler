Plot

In 1996, in the original Independence Day, a fierce race of aliens attacked Earth, in an attempt to completely exterminate the human
race. The aliens destroyed every major city around the world. David Levinson discovered some of their technologies and their weaknesses.
An all-out assault ensued, the alien mothership was destroyed, and the aliens were defeated. Now, 20 years later, the alien threat has
returned. The alien queen had somehow escaped the mothership's destruction and returned to their homeworld. David Levinson, now
a top notch researcher, discovered a distress signal coming from one of the downed alien ships. Their call for help was answered...
